Our Philosophy

A Café Carved from
Wood & Wonder

House of Appetite was born from a single idea — food should feel like a journey. Tucked in Matunga's VSNL Colony, our warm wooden interiors, arched botanical windows, and open kitchen create a space where Asian fusion meets desi soul.

From hand-folded dumplings to slow-simmered ramen, every dish is crafted with intention. We call it comfort with character.
